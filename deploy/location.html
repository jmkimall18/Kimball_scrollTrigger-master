Where we are

More than just a hotel, the Fairmont Le Chateau Frontenac is an icon in the Quebec City. It has been perched on top Cape Diamond for over a century overlooking the St. Lawrence River and the Suffern Terrace. You can view the best sights from the Old Port (des Canotiers), Observatoire de la Capitale, Citadelle de Quebec, and the Levis (Across the river) from the terrace.

About Us

The location of the hotel is perfect for anybody to explore the attractive and popular sites of the Quebec city on foot. The Fairmont is only a few blocks away from a lot of the city’s tourist attractions like the Citadelle, Plains of Abraham, shops, museums, and restaurants.The hotel has 611 guestrooms and offers a large variety of services such as the Afternoon Tea Service, four restaurants, several exhibits, an art gallery, health club and spa, and even a ski shuttle.

How to Get Here

With the hotel being located in the middle of the city, there are plenty of transportation services available. There is easy access to the airport, train, and we can offer a luxury limousine service if you so choose.

Airport

Driving from the Jean-Lesage Airport to Fairmont Le Château Frontenac takes approximately 20 minutes. Taxi services available from the airport at a flat rate of $35.10.

Train Service

Via Rail: 0.6 miles (1 km) from the hotel (in Lower Town) The Bus Depot is also at the same location. No shuttle service. Taxi service is approximately $6 to the station

Limousine Service

Arrangements for a limousine can be made directly with the concierge desk. We offer year-round shuttle service operated by Limousine A1, available between Quebec Jean Lesage International Airport (YQB) and Fairmont Le Château Frontenac. Charges are applicable.
